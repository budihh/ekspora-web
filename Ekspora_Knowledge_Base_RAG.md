## PILAR 2: TRADE & OPERATIONAL POLICIES

> *(Standard international trade parameters applicable to all Ekspora B2B transactions.)*

### 2.1 Shipping & Logistics Terms (Incoterms)

As an international trading company, Ekspora prioritizes logistical flexibility and security for every buying partner.

- **Standard Incoterm:** **FOB (Free On Board)** from main ports in Indonesia, namely **Tanjung Priok (Jakarta)** or **Tanjung Perak (Surabaya)**.
- **Alternative Incoterm:** **CIF (Cost, Insurance, and Freight)** schemes can be negotiated, adjusted to the shipping route and the buyer's destination country.

### 2.2 Terms of Payment

To guarantee the security of international B2B transactions, Ekspora accepts the following payment methods:

- **T/T (Telegraphic Transfer):** Payment via bank transfer, with a Down Payment system according to contract agreements.
- **L/C (Letter of Credit):** Irrevocable L/C at sight issued by a highly reputable international bank.

### 2.3 Additional Services & Export Documentation

- **Sample Delivery:** Ekspora facilitates product sample delivery for international prospective buyers. The sample delivery cost is borne by the buyer.
- **Standard Export Documentation:** Every large-scale cargo shipment is always accompanied by official export documents, including **Certificate of Origin** and **Phytosanitary Certificate** (specifically for agricultural commodities, according to the destination country's requirements).
